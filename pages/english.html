#1 receive. Always followed by an object

#2 become. Always followed by an adjective

#3 understand. Always followed by a pronoun. Lo prenderei solo come un modo di dire. get something (idiomatic)

#4 answer or respond to a ringing phone or someone who's at the door. Lo prenderei solo come un modo di dire

#5 buy. Always followed by an object. Rafforzativo per ottenere

#6 retrieve something(and, often, to bring or take it or them to another place or person). Always followed by a pronouns. Lo prenderei solo come un modo di dire
